Master the Craft of Mead

Join our master mead-makers for hands-on workshops. Learn the chemistry, the history, and the art of fermentation.

Beginner Level

Mead Making 101

Everything you need to know to start your first batch at home. We cover honey selection, yeast hydration, sanitation, and basic equipment.

What You'll Learn:
  • Floral sources & honey varietals
  • Basic fermentation science
  • Using a hydrometer for precision
  • Bottling & Aging techniques
Next Class: June 15th $85 / person
Advanced Level

Botanical Infusions

Take your mead to the next level by mastering the art of metheglins and melomels. Learn how to balance spices and fruits without overpowering the honey.

What You'll Learn:
  • Fruit processing for fermentation
  • Tincture vs. Direct addition of spices
  • Back-sweetening & Balancing acidity
  • Oak aging & Tannin management
Next Class: July 2nd $120 / person

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to bring my own equipment?

No, we provide all the necessary tools and ingredients for the workshop. You'll even leave with a 1-gallon fermenter and your first batch started!

Is there an age requirement?

Yes, all participants must be 21 years of age or older as tasting is an integral part of the curriculum.

What is your cancellation policy?

Full refunds are available up to 72 hours before the class. After that, we can offer credit toward a future workshop.
