Melomel

Cherry Blossom Melomel

$39.00

Inspired by the fleeting beauty of spring. This Melomel features tart Montmorency cherries fermented with a light, floral blossom honey. The result is a pale pink mead with a delicate almond-like aroma and a bright, fruity finish that is perfect for pairing with fresh goat cheese or summer salads.

Tasting Notes

Aroma: Tart cherry, almond, and soft white flowers.

Palate: Light-bodied, crisp acidity, and a subtle stone-fruit sweetness.

Finish: Clean, dry, and slightly floral.
