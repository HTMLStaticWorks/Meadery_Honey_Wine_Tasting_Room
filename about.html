Crafting Liquid History

Founded in 2012 by master fermenter Arthur Sterling, Nectar Meadery was born out of a passion for historical beverages and a deep respect for the natural world. What started in a small garage with two beehives has grown into an artisan cellar dedicated to the world's oldest fermented drink.

Our philosophy is simple: source the finest raw honey, treat it with minimal heat, and allow time to do the rest. We believe that mead should reflect the seasonal blooms of our local landscape.

Local First

100% of our honey comes from within a 50-mile radius.
